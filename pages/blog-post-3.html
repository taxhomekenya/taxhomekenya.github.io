Key Deadlines for 2026

Mark these important dates on your calendar:

  • Monthly: VAT returns (20th of following month)
  • Monthly: PAYE returns (9th of following month)
  • Quarterly: Estimated tax payments (if applicable)
  • June 30: Individual income tax returns deadline
  • August 31: Company income tax returns deadline
  • Throughout the year: Maintain records and respond to KRA inquiries

Common Compliance Mistakes to Avoid

  • Late Filing: Missing deadlines can result in significant penalties
  • Inaccurate Records: Ensure all financial information is correct and complete
  • Inadequate Documentation: Keep supporting documents for all transactions
  • Incorrect Tax Calculations: Double-check all tax calculations
  • Failure to Update Information: Keep your KRA registration current
  • Ignoring KRA Notices: Always respond to KRA communications promptly
