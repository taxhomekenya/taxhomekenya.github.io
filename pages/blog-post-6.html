Cash Flow Is King in Business

By Tax Home Kenya Limited

Many businesses are making profits… but still struggling financially.

Why?

👉 Profit is not cash.

You can record high sales and still:

❌ Struggle to pay bills

❌ Delay salaries

❌ Depend on loans

📊 The Real Issue? Cash Flow.

In today's economy:

📌 Customers delay payments

📌 Expenses are rising

📌 Credit sales are increasing

👉 Result: Money on paper, but no cash in hand.

✅ What Should You Do?

✔️ Track cash weekly — know exactly where your money is

✔️ Follow up on receivables — don't let debts age

✔️ Reduce credit sales — tighten your credit terms

✔️ Build a cash reserve — prepare for lean months

📖 "The plans of the diligent lead surely to abundance…"

— Proverbs 21:5

✨ Don't just aim for profit — manage your cash wisely.
